DescriptionThis dress was for afternoon events or ceremonies, possibly worn for a graduation, over a slip. The ruffles and self sash give it a soft look, but it is made of stiff silk organdy. The sleeves are cut in one with the bodice (kimono sleeve). The bodice has little shaping.
