Properties

Date1925
DescriptionDress of sheer ivory cotton batiste: boatneck; cap sleeves; dropped waist with skirt gathered and hand sewn to waistline at sides; mid calf length; metal hook and eye closures; inserted lace panels from neckline to hemline at center front and center back; lace panel trim along entire hemline; self bias tape hand sewn to edges of neck and sleeves; vertical lace panels machine stitched; hand embroidered flowers. Worn in 1925 for the Daisy Chain at Vassar Commencement by Kathryn Keeler Sherrill VC '27.
